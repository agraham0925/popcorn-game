Popcorn Game

How to Play:

Begin by selecting 1 to 2 players. Each round, collect as many popcorn pieces as you can in your bucket. Each popcorn piece collected in the bucket gives you 1 point. Collect any unpopped kernels, lose 1-3 points (point values for unpopped kernels are random!). Player with the most points at the end of 5 rounds wins!
