Popcorn Game

How to Play:

Each round, collect as many popcorn pieces as you can in your bucket. Each popcorn piece collected in the bucket gives you 1 point. Collect any unpopped kernels, lose 1-3 points (point values for unpopped kernels are random!). Game can be played with 1-2 players. Be sure to click "2 Players" below before start. Player with the most points at the end of 3 rounds wins!
